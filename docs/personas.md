# Personas — CTOC Command

## Primary — Tier-1 SOC Analyst ("M. Okafor · T2")
- **Role / context:** Front-line triage on shift; lives in the alert queue; high alert volume,
  strict SLAs.
- **Goals:** Triage fast and correctly; escalate the right things; not miss a real intrusion in
  the noise.
- **Frustrations:** Pivoting across six consoles; duplicate/low-value alerts; unclear "what now".
- **What success feels like:** A fused, ranked stream where one click opens full context
  (the Triage Drawer) and the obvious next action is right there.

## Primary — Tier-2 / Incident Response Lead
- **Role / context:** Owns active incidents; scopes blast radius; coordinates containment.
- **Goals:** Understand campaign shape (kill-chain stage, affected assets, lateral paths) and
  drive response with playbooks.
- **Frustrations:** Reconstructing the timeline from scattered tools; unclear ownership/SLA state.
- **What success feels like:** Kill-chain + blast-radius topology + MITRE coverage in one view;
  incidents with clear stage, lead, and SLA.

## Secondary — Fraud Operations Analyst ("L. Mendez")
- **Role / context:** Reviews held transactions, corridor risk, and ML anomaly clusters.
- **Goals:** Hold/clear suspicious money movement quickly; minimize false positives and loss.
- **What success feels like:** A prioritized hold queue with score, corridor, and detector in one
  table; fraud loss-vs-prevented trend.

## Secondary — CISO / Executive
- **Role / context:** Accountable for posture; briefs the board and regulators.
- **Goals:** A truthful, at-a-glance posture; loss-avoided vs. actual; regulatory readiness.
- **What success feels like:** A board-ready Executive Overview with a one-line headline,
  ranked top risks, and framework readiness (NIST/ISO/PCI/DORA/NYDFS).

## Supporting — Insider Threat / HR-Legal reviewer
- **Role / context:** Joint review of behavioral/UEBA anomalies under ethical scoping.
- **Goals:** Surface privileged-access and departing-employee risk without overreach; coordinate
  with HR/Legal on sensitive cases.
